Conversion Goal:
Taken after a try has been scored where the kicker will attempt to kick the ball between the uprights above the cross bar. The kick is taken inline with where the ball was touch down.
CONVERSION = +2pts


Penalty Goal:
A player scores by kicking a goal from a penalty kick. Penalty kicks are awarded when an opposing player commits and offence.
PENALTY = +3pts


Dropped Goal:
Scored during play when an attacking player kicks a goal from a drop kick. A drop kick is when the ball is dropped from the hands to the ground and kicked as it rises from its first bounce. The ball must pass between the uprights and over the crossbar to score.
DROPPED GOAL = +3pts
