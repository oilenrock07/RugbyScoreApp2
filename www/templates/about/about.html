Having two sons play club and school rugby from the age of 7, Chris Bigsby decided it was time to develop this app as many parents, including himself, could barely recall the score during a game let alone a year later. Rugby Score Tracker is an aid for parents, coaches and teachers to keep track of the score during games and if you have downloaded the Pro version you can record the scores for future reference. Note: Rugby Score Tracker is not an official score keeper and match officials score recordings are final. Respect the Referee

If purchasing the Rugby Score Tracker Pro you will have access to historic games, list of clubs played and a full break down of scores and teams. You will be able to share scores with friends and family via email and social media.
